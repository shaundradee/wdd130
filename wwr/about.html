Splash Whitewater Rafting

Our Purpose: At Splash Whitewater Rafting, we are all about good times, wild rivers, and real adventure. Our mission is to help people unplug, paddle hard, and make awesome memories—while keeping things safe, fun, and friendly every step of the way.

History

From the beginning, we've been committed to providing safe, unforgettable experiences on the water, with a focus on environmental responsibility and creating lasting memories. Over the years, we have expanded our offered adventures, all while keeping our dedication to safety and the thrill of adventure at the forefront.

Today, Splash Whitewater Rafting is proud to be a trusted name in outdoor adventure, welcoming rafters of all skill levels to explore the beauty and power of the river. We are still driven by the same passion that started it all—making the river experience accessible, exciting, and unforgettable for everyone.
